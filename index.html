About

I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City (SKL-IOTSC), University of Macau, advised by Prof. Li Li and Prof. Chengzhong Xu (IEEE Fellow). I am currently a Visiting Researcher at the Machine Learning Systems Group, University of Cambridge, working with Prof. Nicholas D. Lane. I also collaborate with Dr. Zhan Shi and Prof. Zhijiang Guo.

My research focuses on building full-stack edge intelligence systems — spanning algorithms, systems, and hardware — for real-world deployment of foundation models under resource constraints.

  • Efficient Foundation Models for Edge Deployment.
  • Distributed & Collaborative Edge AI Systems.
  • Hardware-Software Co-Design for Real-World AI Applications.

I have published 20+ papers in top-tier journals and conferences, including MICRO, USENIX ATC, TPDS, NeurIPS, and ICML, with several selected for Oral presentations.
