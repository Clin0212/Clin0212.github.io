I am a third-year PhD student in Computer Science at State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City (SKL-IOTSC), University of Macau under the supervision of Prof. Li Li and Prof. Chengzhong Xu. I am also fortunate to be mentored by and to collaborate closely with Dr. Zhan Shi and Prof. Zhijiang Guo. Before joining UM, I received my BEng degree from Xi’an Jiaotong University in 2022. I am broadly interested in ML for System. My current research interests focus on Efficient LLMs and On-device Learning.
