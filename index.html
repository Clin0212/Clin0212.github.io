Short Bio

👋 I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City (SKL-IOTSC), University of Macau, advised by Prof. Li Li and Prof. Chengzhong Xu (IEEE Fellow). Currently, I am a Visiting Researcher at the Machine Learning Systems Group, University of Cambridge, working with Prof. Nicholas D. Lane. I also collaborate closely with Dr. Zhan Shi and Prof. Zhijiang Guo. Previously, I obtained my B.Eng. from Xi’an Jiaotong University in 2022.

💡 Research Interests: I specialize in ML for Systems and Edge AI.

My work focuses on software–hardware co-design to address stringent SWaP (Size, Weight, and Power) constraints for foundation models on edge devices. I have published 20+ papers in top-tier venues, including several Oral presentations. Currently, I am exploring edge reasoning and healthcare applications.
