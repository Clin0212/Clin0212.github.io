Short Bio

👋 I am a final-year PhD student in Computer Science at the State Key Laboratory of Internet of Things for Smart City (SKL-IOTSC), University of Macau, under the supervision of Prof. Li Li and Prof. Chengzhong Xu (IEEE Fellow). Currently, I am a visiting researcher at the Machine Learning Systems Group, University of Cambridge, under the supervision of Prof. Nicholas D. Lane. I am also fortunate to be mentored by and collaborate closely with Dr. Zhan Shi and Prof. Zhijiang Guo. Before joining UM, I received my BEng degree from Xi’an Jiaotong University in 2022.

💡 I am broadly interested in ML for System. My research focuses on efficient machine learning systems and edge AI systems. I specialize in full-stack software–hardware co-design, spanning from algorithms to systems, to address the stringent SWaP constraints in deploying foundation models on edge devices. On these topics, I have published 20+ papers in leading venues, with several selected for Oral presentations. Currently, I am particularly interested in edge reasoning and edge-based healthcare applications.
